Restaurant Essential

Grease Trap Services

Professional cleaning, pumping, and maintenance for restaurant and commercial kitchen grease traps. We help you stay compliant with health department regulations and prevent costly blockages that can shut down your kitchen.

  • Grease trap cleaning & pumping
  • Health code compliance
  • Scheduled maintenance plans
  • Grease interceptor installation
  • Regulatory documentation
High Pressure

Drain Cleaning & Hydro-Jetting

High-pressure water jetting and professional drain cleaning to clear blockages and prevent backups in commercial drain systems. Our hydro-jetting equipment delivers up to 4,000 PSI — removing grease, scale, roots, and debris without damaging pipes.

  • Hydro-jetting (up to 4,000 PSI)
  • Commercial drain snaking
  • Root intrusion removal
  • Video inspection
  • Preventative drain programs
Installation & Repair

Water Heater Services

Installation, repair, and maintenance of commercial water heaters, boilers, and tankless systems to ensure reliable hot water supply. We work with all major brands and can handle systems of any size for hotels, restaurants, and large commercial properties.

  • Commercial water heater installation
  • Tankless system installation
  • Boiler repair & maintenance
  • Water heater replacement
  • Energy efficiency upgrades

  • City report submission
  • Multi-property management
